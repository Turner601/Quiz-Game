Quiz Game

The quiz consists of 15 random questions. Each question will have 4 answers to choose from which only 1 of the 4 will be the correct answer. After choosing your answer, click on the "Next Question" button to move on. Once you are done with the quiz, click on the "Submit Quiz" button to see your score. Must get 8 or more correct to pass and less than 8 is a fail. Once you have chosen your answer that will be your final answer so choose wisely.

You have now finished the quiz, click the button below to submit it and reveal your score
